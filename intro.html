Introduction    
  This is an interactive tour of a small conference center located in Reykjavik Iceland, designed by Alvar Aalto and built during 1965-1968. The building was a gift from the governments of all the Nordic countries. It contains a hall for conferences and exhibitions, a library with a Nordic collection, a number of meeting rooms and a cafe.  
 
Virtual Tour    
  Included in this website is an interactive virtual tour. You will be able to place yourself at various locations in the hall and look around to get a better sense of the spatial inventiveness of Alvar Aalto.
